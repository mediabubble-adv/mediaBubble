# Keyword Research — Arabic SEO for Egyptian Market

## Masri vs MSA Keyword Strategy

### When to Target MSA Keywords

| Scenario | Example | Volume | Competition |
|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Head terms, broadest reach | دكتور في مصر, مستشفى في القاهرة | High | High |
| Official/professional queries | استشارة طبية, علاج الأسنان, تأمين صحي | High | Medium |
| Government-related | التأمين الصحي الشامل, وزارة الصحة | Medium | Low |
| Academic/research terms | آخر أبحاث السرطان, علاج السكري | Low | Medium |

### When to Target Masri Keywords

| Scenario | Example | Volume | Competition |
|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Voice search / conversational | عاوز دكتور كويس, فين أحسن مستشفى | Medium | Low |
| Local intent | دكتور نضيف في المعادي, مستشفى قريب مني | High | Low |
| Problem/symptom queries | عندي الم في ضهري, كل شوية تعبان | High | Very Low |
| Review/comparison | مين أحسن دكتور, تجربتي مع مستشفى | Medium | Very Low |
| How-to / practical | ازاي أعمل كشف, طريقة حجز موعد | Medium | Low |

**Key insight:** Masri keywords have dramatically lower competition while matching real user search intent. Google's BERT and MUM updates understand natural language queries, making Masri long-tail keywords highly effective.

## Keyword Categorization Framework

| Category | Query Type | Masri Example | Suggested Content |
|----------|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Informational | Conditions & symptoms | الم في ضهري يعني ايه | Article, guide, video |

## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keywords (MSA + Masri)
Generate 20-30 seed keywords from:
- Brand services and specialities
- Egyptian health concerns (seasonal + chronic)
- Competitor keywords (Vezeeta, El-Doctoor, Daily Medical)
- Google autocomplete (search in Arabic with Masri spelling)

### Step 2: Expand Using Tools
| Tool | Arabic Support | Use Case |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Google Keyword Planner | Good (MSA) | Volume data, competition |
| Ahrefs | Good (MSA) | Keyword difficulty, competitor gaps |
| SEMRush | Good (MSA + partial Masri) | Full keyword analysis |
| Google Search Console | Good (MSA + Masri) | Actual queries driving traffic |
| Google Trends (Egypt) | Good (MSA + Masri slang) | Seasonal trends, rising queries |
| AnswerThePublic (Arabic) | Good | Question-based keywords |
| Ubersuggest | Partial Arabic | Quick exploration |

### Step 3: Categorize by Intent
Map each keyword to:
- **Funnel stage** (Awareness → Consideration → Conversion)
- **Content type** (Article, page, video, guide)
- **Platform** (Website, YouTube, Google Business)
- **Priority** (High/Medium/Low based on volume × relevance × competition)

### Step 4: Cluster into Topic Groups
Group keywords into topics with a pillar page and supporting content:
```
Topic: دكتور أسنان في مصر
├── Pillar: دليل أطباء الأسنان في مصر (comprehensive guide)
├── Sub: ازاي تختار دكتور أسنان (how-to guide)
├── Sub: أسعار تركيبات الأسنان في مصر (pricing page)
├── Sub: تجربتي مع دكتور أسنان (testimonial)
└── Sub: أعراض مشاكل الأسنان (symptom guide)
```

## Egyptian Search Behavior Patterns

### Mobile-First
- 90%+ of Arabic searches in Egypt are on mobile
- Voice search is growing rapidly among 18-35 demographic
- Optimize for "near me" queries in Masri (قريب مني, جنبي, في منطقتي)

### Time-Based Patterns
| Time | Search Behavior | Keyword Opportunity |
|------|----------------|---------------------|
| Morning (7-10 AM) | Symptom checking, pharmacy hours | الم في, دواء لـ, صيدلية مفتوحة |
| Midday (12-3 PM) | Booking appointments, directions | حجز دكتور, مستشفى قريب, دكتور في |
| Evening (7-11 PM) | Research, comparisons, reviews | أحسن, مين أفضل, تقييم, تجربتي مع |
| Weekends (Fri-Sat) | Cosmetic procedures, non-urgent | دكتور تجميل, عملية, نضارة بشرة |
| Ramadan nights | Nutritional, digestive, sleep issues | أكل صحي في رمضان, نوم رمضان, هضم |

### Egyptian Spelling Variations
| Standard Arabic | Masri Common Spelling | SEO Approach |
|----------------|----------------------|--------------|
| ماذا | إيه, ايه | Include both in content |
| أين | فين | Target in long-tail |
| كيف | إزاي, ازاي | Target in long-tail |
| أريد | عايز, عاوز | Target in long-tail |
| متى | إمتى, امتى | Target in long-tail |
| جيد | كويس | Target explicitly (high search volume) |
| جميل | نضيف | Target explicitly |
| قريب | قرّب | Include both |
| أيضا | كمان | Target in conversational queries |

## Keyword Priority Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| ★★★ | High volume (500+/mo), Low competition, High relevance | Create pillar content immediately |
| ★★☆ | Medium volume (100-500/mo), Low-medium competition, High relevance | Create supporting content |
| ★☆☆ | Low volume (<100/mo), Low competition, Relevant to niche | Include in existing content, monitor |
| — | High volume, High competition | Long-term play, build authority first |

## Monthly Keyword Review

Track these metrics per keyword:
- Current ranking position
- Search volume trend (up/down/stable)
- Click-through rate from search
- Competitor ranking changes
- New keyword opportunities discovered
