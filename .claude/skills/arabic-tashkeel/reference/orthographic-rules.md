# Arabic Tashkeel — Orthographic Rules Quick Reference

## Hamzat al-Wasl vs Hamzat al-Qat'

| Type | Written | Occurs In | Example |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Hamzat al-wasl (وصل) | ا (alif without hamza) | Definite article ال, past commands, some nouns | اذْهَبْ (go!), اسْم (name) |
| Hamzat al-qat' (قطع) | أ / إ (alif with hamza) | Most verbs, most nouns | أَكَلَ (he ate), إِنْسَان (human) |

## Alif Maqsura (ى) vs Alif Mamduda (ا)

| Ending | Used For | Example |
|--------|----------|---------|
| ى (alif maqsura) | Third-weak verbs, some nouns | رَمَى (he threw), مُسْتَشْفَى (hospital) |
| ا (alif mamduda) | Regular alif in most contexts | كِتَاب (book), جَمَال (beauty) |

## Ta' Marbuta (ة) vs Ta' Maftuha (ت)

| Form | Used For | Example | In Construct/Plural |
|------|----------|---------|---------------------|
| ة (hā') | Feminine singular nouns/adjectives | مَدْرَسَة (school) | مَدْرَسَةُ (nominative construct) |
| ت (tā') | Feminine plural (-āt), some words | بَنَات (girls) | Always remains ت |

## Shadda (تشديد) Rules

| Context | Shadda Required | Example |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| Double consonant | Yes | شَدَّ (he pulled hard) |
| Form II verbs | Always on middle radical | عَلَّمَ (he taught) |
| Form V verbs | Always | تَعَلَّمَ (he learned) |
| Form II verbal nouns | Always on middle radical | تَعْلِيم (teaching) |
