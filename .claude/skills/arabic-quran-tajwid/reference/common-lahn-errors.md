# Tajwid — Common Lahn (Error) Correction Guide

## Jali (Major/Apparent Errors — Sinful)

| Error | Why It's Wrong | Correct Recitation | Rule |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|------|
| Replacing ص with س | Changes meaning (صِراط vs. سِراط) | Sharp ص, raised tongue | Check ص مخرج: tongue tip to front palate with qalqala |
| Not pronouncing hamza | Smooth as 'ayn | Strong glottal stop | Practice hamza in isolation before joining |
| Missing ghunnah on ن or م | Nasalization absent | 2-count nasal for ن and م mushaddad | "إنَّ" → ghunnah must be perceptible |
| Dropping final vowel | Always stopping without harakah | Follow waqf rules for each letter | Only certain letters get sukoon in waqf |
| Pronouncing ض as ظ | Very common error | ض is lateral (side of tongue); ظ is tip | "الضالين" — ض is distinctive Arabic sound |

## Khafi (Minor/Subtle Errors — Makruh)

| Error | Correction | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Over-emphasis of ر | Reduce tafkhīm level | Tafkhīm of ر has 3 levels — use lighter in "رب" vs "رجل" |
| Under-emphasis of heavy ل in الله | Full tafkhīm of ل | "الله" → emphatic ل (except after kasrah: "لله") |
| ل م without idghām | Apply idghām rules | "عَن مَا" → "عَمَّا" (iqlāb for م) |
| Slight vowel added in madd | Maintain pure 2/4/6 | No diphthongization in prolongation |
| Flat ghunnah | Add resonance | Ghunnah should exit from khayshūm (nasal passage) |

## Correction Drills

```
Drill 1: ص vs س
— صبر (ṣabr) vs سبر (sabr)
— صورة (ṣūra) vs سورة (sūra) — DIFFERENT MEANING!
— Exercise: Hold ص for 2 seconds at makhraj

Drill 2: ض vs ظ
— ضل (ḍalla) vs ظل (ẓalla) — DIFFERENT MEANING!
— ض Exercise: Say "ض" holding side of tongue to molars
— ظ Exercise: Say "ظ" with tongue tip to teeth

Drill 3: Ghunnah
— إنَّ — hold nasalization for 2 counts
— آمَنَّا — double ghunnah on نَّ
— من وال — apply iqlāb: م → م with ghunnah
```
