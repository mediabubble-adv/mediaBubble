# Islamic Observances — Ramadan Content & Media Guide

## Content Strategy Throughout Ramadan

| Period | Audience Mood | Content Type | Do's | Don'ts |
|--------|--------------|--------------|------|--------|
| Week 1 | High spiritual energy; energized | Religious (motivational), Iftar recipes, community iftar invites | "Ramadan Kareem"; ibadah goals; prayer schedules | Too many ads; entertainment-first content |
| Mid (wk 2–3) | Fatigue; routine settling | Short-form; reminder content; evening activities | Light motivation; sleep tips; charity storytelling | Long articles; complex calls-to-action |
| Last 10 days | Peak ibadah; night prayers | Laylat al-Qadr content; duas; zakat reminders | Laylat al-Qadr (best night); itikaf; sadaqah | Ignoring last 10 days (most impactful) |
| Last 3 days | Eid anticipation; shopping | Eid prep; zakat al-fitr; Eid greetings | "Zakat al-Fitr due"; "Eid outfit ideas" | Last-minute "Ramadan specials" (reads as desperate) |
| Eid day | Celebration | Congratulations; family content | "عيد مبارك"; "تقبل الله منا ومنكم" | Selling; work-related content (tone deaf) |

## Sensitive Marketing During Ramadan

| Category | Guidance |
|----------|----------|
| Food ads before Iftar | Acceptable but avoid excessive/host food shaming (تكسير) |
| Perfume/cosmetics | Acceptable; emphasize moderation (not haram if not worn for non-mahram) |
| Music | Content with instrumental music only (no vocals except nasheeds) for conservative audiences |
| Entertainment | Avoid risqué movies/shows; emphasis on family-friendly anthems |
| Alcohol/tobacco | Never — even in non-Muslim contexts during Ramadan |
| Sporting events | Acceptable after Iftar; avoid content during prayer times |

## Key Duas & Phrases for Content

| Occasion | Arabic | English |
|----------|--------|---------|
| At Iftar | اللهم لك صمت وعلى رزقك أفطرت | O Allah, I fasted for You and with Your provision I break my fast |
| Laylat al-Qadr | اللهم إنك عفو تحب العفو فاعف عني | O Allah, You are Pardoning, love pardon, so pardon me |
| Eid greeting | تقبل الله منا ومنكم صالح الأعمال | May Allah accept from us and you our good deeds |
| Early Ramadan | كل عام وأنتم بخير | Every year and you are well |
