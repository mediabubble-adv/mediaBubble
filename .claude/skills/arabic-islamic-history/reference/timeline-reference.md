# Arabic Islamic History — Timeline Reference

## Pre-Islamic & Early Islamic (to 661 CE)

| Date | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|-------------|
| ~570 CE | Year of the Elephant; birth of Prophet Muhammad | End of pre-Islamic era |
| 610 CE | First revelation at Cave Hira | Beginning of Islam |
| 622 CE | Hijrah to Medina | Start of Islamic calendar |
| 630 CE | Conquest of Mecca | Unification of Arabian Peninsula |
| 632–634 | Caliphate of Abu Bakr | Ridda Wars; compilation of Quran |
| 634–644 | Caliphate of Umar ibn al-Khattab | Conquest of Levant, Egypt, Persia |
| 644–656 | Caliphate of Uthman ibn Affan | Standardization of Quranic text |
| 656–661 | Caliphate of Ali ibn Abi Talib | First Fitna (civil war); rise of sects |

## Umayyad Caliphate (661–750 CE)

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| 661 | Mu'awiyah establishes Umayyad rule in Damascus |
| 680 | Battle of Karbala; Husayn ibn Ali martyred |
| 691 | Dome of the Rock completed in Jerusalem |
| 711 | Conquest of Spain (Andalusia) and Sindh (India) |
| 750 | Abbasid Revolution; fall of Umayyads |

## Abbasid Caliphate (750–1258 CE)

| Period | Event |
|--------|-------|
| 750–800 | Establishment of Baghdad (762); rise of Barmakids |
| 786–809 | Harun al-Rashid; peak of Golden Age |
| 830 | Bayt al-Hikma established in Baghdad |
| 9th–10th c. | Translation movement; al-Khwarizmi, al-Kindi, al-Farabi |
| 945–1055 | Buyid domination of Baghdad |
| 1055–1194 | Seljuk Turkish domination |
| 1099 | First Crusade captures Jerusalem |
| 1187 | Saladin recaptures Jerusalem |
| 1258 | Mongol sack of Baghdad; Abbasid Caliphate ends |

## Mamluk & Ottoman (1250–1922 CE)

| Period | Event |
|--------|-------|
| 1250–1517 | Mamluk Sultanate in Egypt and Levant |
| 1516–1517 | Ottoman conquest of Egypt and Syria |
| 1534 | Ottoman conquest of Iraq |
| 1798 | Napoleon invades Egypt |
| 1805 | Muhammad Ali becomes ruler of Egypt |
| 1830–1912 | French colonization of North Africa |
| 1882 | British occupation of Egypt |
| 1916 | Arab Revolt against Ottomans |
| 1922 | End of Ottoman Empire |
