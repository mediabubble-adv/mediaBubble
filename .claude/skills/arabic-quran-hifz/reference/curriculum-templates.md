# Hifz — Curriculum Templates

## Standard Daily Plan (1–2 hours)

| Time | Component | Duration | Content |
|------|-----------|----------|---------|
| 0–15 min | Review (مراجعة) | 15 min | Recite 1 juz' of old memorization — preferably at dawn |
| 15–30 min | New memorization (حفظ جديد) | 15 min | New material: 7–10 lines (half a page) |
| 30–45 min | Repetition (تكرار) | 15 min | Repeat new material 10–20 times by phrase |
| 45–60 min | Linking (ربط) | 15 min | Connect new material to previous ayah |
| 60–90 min | Audio recording check | 30 min | Record self, compare to your riwayah |

## Intensive Plan (Ramadan or Summer — 4 hours)

| Block | Duration | Content |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Fajr | 60 min | Review old — 2 juz' |
| Morning | 60 min | New material — 15–20 lines (1 page) |
| Dhuhr | 30 min | Audio test: read new material to QA |
| Afternoon | 60 min | Repetition: 20× new, 10× surrounding lines |
| Maghrib | 30 min | 5-minute test from QA on new material |

## Milestone Schedule

| Level | Target | Daily New | Timeframe |
|-------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Beginner | Juz' 1 (ʿAmma) | 3–5 lines | 2 months |
| Foundation | Juz' 1–5 (Tabārak) | 5–7 lines | 1 year |
| Midway | Juz' 1–15 | 7–10 lines | 2 years |
| Complete | Full Quran (30 juz') | 10 lines | 3 years |

## Common Pacing

| Pace | Daily New | Complete in |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Standard (Medina method) | 5–7 lines | 3–4 years |
| Intensive (Egyptian ma'ahad) | 15–20 lines | 2–2.5 years |
| Mashyakha (1-on-1) | 10 lines | 3 years |
| Self-study + audio | 7–10 lines | 3–4 years |
