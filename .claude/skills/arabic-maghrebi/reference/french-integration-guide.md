# Maghrebi Darija — French Integration Guide

## When French Is Appropriate

| Domain | French Word | Accepted In | Example |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| Technology | l'ordinateur (لورديناتور) | Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia | "عندي مشكلة فـ l'ordinateur" |
| Office | le bureau / le rendez-vous | All Maghreb | "عندي un rendez-vous مع المدير" |
| Education | le baccalauréat (الباك) | All | "جاب ليباك" (got their bac) |
| Healthcare | l'hôpital / la clinique | All | "راه فـ l'hôpital" |
| Transport | le taxi / le bus / le métro | Morocco, Algeria | "ناخذ le bus" |
| Clothing | la veste / la chemise | All | "شرات la veste هادي" |
| Food | le pain / la mayonnaise | All | "عطيني le pain" |

## When NOT to Use French

| Situation | Reason | Use Instead |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Religion | French feels colonial | 100% Arabic: الصلاة، القرآن، المسجد |
| Family names | Offensive to mispronounce | Arabic only: أمي، بابا، خالتي |
| Intimate emotions | French feels distant | Arabic: بحبك، وحشتيني |
| Business formal | French too casual | Arabic or code-switch to formal French |
| Patriotism/national identity | Political sensitivity | Full Darija or full Arabic |
