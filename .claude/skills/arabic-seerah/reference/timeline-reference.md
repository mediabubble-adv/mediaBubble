# Seerah — Chronological Timeline Reference

## Pre-Prophethood (Year Zero = year of the Elephant / 570 CE)

| Year | Age | Event |
|------|-----|-------|
| Year of Elephant | 0 | Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) born in Mecca — Monday, 12 Rabi' al-Awwal |
| 575 | 5 | Halimah al-Sa'diyah returns him to Aminah |
| 576 | 6 | Mother Aminah dies in al-Abwa |
| 578 | 8 | Grandfather Abdul Muttalib dies |
| 580 | 10 | Under uncle Abu Talib's care |
| 583 | 13 | Journey to Syria with Abu Talib; Bahira the monk recognizes prophethood |
| 590 | 20 | Allegiance to Hilf al-Fudul (league of justice) |
| 595 | 25 | Marriage to Khadijah (age 40) — she proposes through Nafisah |
| 598 | 28 | Birth of first child: Qasim |
| 605 | 35 | Black Stone placement (Hajar al-Aswad) — Muhammad arbitrates |
| 610 | 40 | **First revelation**: Cave Hira, Surah al-Alaq 96:1–5 |

## Meccan Period (610–622 CE / 13 years)

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 610 | First revelation; Khadijah, Ali, Abu Bakr, Waraqah confirm |
| 613 | Public call: "وَأَنذِرْ عَشِيرَتَكَ الْأَقْرَبِينَ" |
| 614 | First migration to Abyssinia (11 men, 4 women) |
| 615 | Second migration to Abyssinia (83 men, 19 women) |
| 616 | Boycott of Quraysh begins — Banu Hashim in Shi'b Abi Talib |
| 619 | **Year of Sorrow**: Khadijah dies (age 65); Abu Talib dies |
| 620 | Journey to Ta'if rejected by Thaqif |
| 621 | **Isra' and Mi'raj** (Night Journey + Ascension) |
| 622 | **First Aqabah Pledge** (12 men from Yathrib) |

## Medinan Period (622–632 CE / 10 years)

| Year (AH) | Event |
|-----------|-------|
| 1 | **Hijrah** to Medina (Yathrib); Masjid Quba built; Brotherhood established |
| 1–2 | Constitution of Medina (الصحيفة) |
| 2 | Direction of prayer changed from Jerusalem to Kaaba |
| 2 (Ramadan) | **Battle of Badr** (17th, 17 March 624) |
| 2 | Zakat prescribed |
| 3 (Shawwal) | **Battle of Uhud** (23 March 625) — Hamzah martyred |
| 4 (Muharram) | Banu Nadir expelled |
| 5 (Dhul Qa'dah) | **Battle of the Trench (Khandaq)** (April 627) |
| 6 | **Treaty of Hudaybiyyah** (March 628) |
| 7 | **Khaybar** conquest; Farewell to Khaybar |
| 7 | Expedition of Mu'tah (severe loss of three commanders) |
| 8 (Ramadan) | **Conquest of Mecca** (January 630) |
| 8 | Battle of Hunayn; Siege of Ta'if |
| 9 | **Year of Deputations** — tribes submit to Islam |
| 9 | Expedition of Tabuk |
| 10 | **Farewell Pilgrimage (Ḥajjat al-Wadāʿ)** — March 632 |
| 11 (Rabi' al-Awwal) | **Death of Prophet (ﷺ)** — Monday, 8 June 632 |
