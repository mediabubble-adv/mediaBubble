# Arabic Art — Contemporary Artists Reference Index

## Major Contemporary Arab Artists

### Painting

| Artist | Country | Born | Known For |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Louay Kayyali | Syria | 1934–1978 | Figurative realism; daily Syrian life |
| Marwan Kassab-Bachi | Syria | 1934–2016 | Abstract expressionist; "the screamer" series |
| Shakir Hassan Al Said | Iraq | 1925–2004 | Founder of Baghdad Modern Art Group; calligraphic abstraction |
| Mohammed Ghani Hikmat | Iraq | 1929–2011 | Public sculpture in Baghdad; "The Unknown Soldier" contributions |
| Mahmoud Said | Egypt | 1897–1964 | Modernist pioneer; Egyptian rural scenes |
| Abdel Hadi el-Gazzar | Egypt | 1925–1966 | Surrealist, folkloric; "The High Dam" series |
| Marwan Sahmoudi | Lebanon | 1941–1999 | Abstract expressionism; gestural |
| Suad al-Attar | Iraq | 1942– | Female artist; surreal landscapes; exile themes |
| Dia al-Azzawi | Iraq | 1939– | Pan-Arab art pioneer; Palestinian cause resonance |
