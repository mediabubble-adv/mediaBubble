# KPI Framework — Content Performance Measurement

## Metric Categories

### Reach Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark (Egypt market) | Tool |
|--------|-----------|------------------------|------|
| Impressions | Total content views | 5K-50K/post (organic) | Meta Business, TikTok Analytics |
| Reach | Unique viewers | 3K-30K/post (organic) | Meta Business |
| Follower growth | New followers/week | 50-500/week (organic) | Platform analytics |
| Share of voice | Your mentions vs competitors | Track monthly | Brand monitoring |
| Website traffic | Monthly unique visitors | 5K-50K/month (early) | Google Analytics |
| Search impressions | Arabic keyword rankings | Track by keyword | Google Search Console |

### Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark (Egypt market) | Tool |
|--------|-----------|------------------------|------|
| Engagement rate | (Likes+comments+shares) / reach | 3-8% (Instagram), 2-5% (Facebook) | Platform analytics |
| Comments | Number of comments per post | 10-100/post | Platform analytics |
| Shares / Saves | Content saves and shares | 50-500/post | Platform analytics |
| Video completion rate | % watched to 75%+ | 20-40% (Reels), 50-70% (TikTok) | Platform analytics |
| Story response rate | Polls, questions, swipe-ups | 5-15% | Instagram Insights |
| Click-through rate | Clicks / impressions | 1-3% (organic), 2-5% (paid) | Platform analytics |

### Conversion Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark | Tool |
|--------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Landing page CTR | Clicks from content to LP | 2-5% | Google Analytics |
| Conversion rate | Bookings / landing page visits | 5-15% | CRM + Analytics |
| Cost per lead (paid) | Spend / leads generated | Track per campaign | Meta Ads, Google Ads |
| Booking value | Average revenue per booking | Track per service | CRM |
| Lead-to-booking rate | % of leads that book | 20-40% | CRM |
| WhatsApp inquiry rate | WhatsApp clicks / reach | 0.5-2% | Meta Business |

### Retention Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark | Tool |
|--------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Repeat booking rate | % of patients who book again | 15-30% | CRM |
| Referral rate | % of bookings from referrals | 10-20% | CRM + survey |
| Email open rate | Opens / delivered | 20-35% (Egypt avg) | Email platform |
| Email click rate | Clicks / opens | 3-8% | Email platform |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score) | Likelihood to recommend | 40-60 | Survey |
| Churn rate | % lost patients/quarter | <10% | CRM |

## Reporting Template

### Weekly Report

```yaml
week_ending: <date>
total_content_published: <number>
total_reach: <organic + paid>
total_engagements: <sum across platforms>
top_performer:
  piece: <content title>
  platform: <platform>
  metric: <engagement rate or reach>
  insight: <why it worked>
bottom_performer:
  piece: <content title>
  metric: <metric>
  lesson: <what to improve>
campaigns_active: <list>
conversions_attributed: <number>
next_week_priority: <focus area>
```

### Monthly Report

```yaml
month: <month>
pillar_performance:
  - pillar_name: <name>
    content_count: <number>
    total_reach: <number>
    avg_engagement_rate: <%>
    top_platform: <platform>
    kpi_progress: <% towards target>
audience_growth:
  instagram: <+/- followers>
  tiktok: <+/- followers>
  facebook: <+/- followers>
  linkedin: <+/- followers>
  youtube: <+/- subscribers>
top_5_pieces:
  - title: <title>
    platform: <platform>
    metric: <value>
    notes: <insight>
conversions:
  bookings: <number>
  leads: <number>
  revenue_attributed: <amount>
  cost_per_lead: <amount>
recommendations:
  - <action item 1>
  - <action item 2>
  - <action item 3>
```

## Quarterly Review Framework

| Question | Data Source |
|----------|------------|
| Which pillars are over/under-performing? | Monthly reports, platform analytics |
| Which platforms give best ROI by content type? | Cost-per-engagement, conversion data |
| What content gaps did competitors exploit? | Competitive audit |
| Which audience segments engaged most? | Platform demographics, CRM data |
| What seasonal trends emerged? | Year-over-year comparison |
| What should we stop / start / continue? | Team retrospective |
