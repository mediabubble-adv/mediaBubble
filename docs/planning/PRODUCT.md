# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** Business owners and marketing leads in Hurghada and the Red Sea (Egypt) and Dubai/UAE. Hotels, resorts, real estate developers, F&B, tourism operators, and local brands evaluating a full-service agency.

**Context:** They research on mobile between meetings or on desktop when comparing vendors. Many are bilingual: English for evaluation, Arabic for trust and local resonance. They need proof of regional expertise, not a generic global agency pitch.

**Job to be done:** Confirm MediaBubble is credible and local, understand which services fit their problem, and start a conversation (contact form, strategy audit, phone, WhatsApp).

**Secondary surface (override register per task):** `apps/brand` is an internal brand-guidelines reference (product register). Default impeccable context targets the public marketing sites unless the task explicitly focuses on the guidelines app.

## Product Purpose

Public marketing websites (`web-eg` at mediabubble.co, `web-ae` at mediabubble.ae) that turn interest into qualified leads. The design is the product: every page must communicate strategic confidence, regional specificity, and craft quality.

Success looks like: clear market positioning (Hurghada vs Dubai), fast trust (client logos, case studies, local proof), frictionless conversion paths, bilingual and RTL excellence, and performance that signals professionalism (fonts, images, PWA, ISR).

## Brand Personality

**Strategic, energetic, locally rooted.** Expert without stiffness; creative without decoration for its own sake. Voice is direct and specific, never aphoristic or vague. Egypt and UAE each get distinct tone and proof, not a single generic "MENA agency" voice.

Emotional goals: confidence ("they know our market"), momentum ("they can execute"), approachability ("easy to start a conversation").

## Anti-references

- Generic digital-agency templates: purple gradients, interchangeable hero metrics, icon-card grids repeated endlessly
- Stock-photo agency tropes and vague promises ("we grow your brand") without logos, cases, or geography
- Treating Egypt and UAE as one interchangeable market
- PDF-style brand books pasted onto the web (decoration over communication)
- AI-slop patterns: gradient text, glassmorphism decor, hero-metric templates, side-stripe borders, modal-first flows

**Reference direction:** Premium regional agency sites with strong typography and proof-led layouts; editorial confidence rather than SaaS-dashboard aesthetics.

## Design Principles

1. **Local credibility first** — market-specific headlines, client logos, footer market switcher, and proof that MediaBubble operates in Hurghada/Dubai, not "everywhere."
2. **Conversion without aggression** — clear CTAs and contact paths; no modal spam or interruptive patterns when inline alternatives work.
3. **Bilingual parity** — Arabic/RTL is first-class (Cairo, `dir`, mirrored layout), not a translated afterthought.
4. **Performance as trust** — fast LCP, optimized images, font strategy, PWA, and polish signal operational competence.
5. **Committed brand color** — navy + yellow from `@mediabubble/design-system`; avoid category-default agency palettes (teal, purple, neon-on-black).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. High-contrast text pairings, visible focus indicators, logical tab order, semantic landmarks (`nav`, `main`, `footer`), descriptive alt text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. RTL reading order and Arabic typography must meet the same bar as English.
