## CTA Replacements

| Current (weak)         | Recommended                               | Why                                    |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| "Learn More"           | "View Case Study" / "See How We Did It"   | Specific destination, sets expectation |
| "Submit"               | "Get My Free Audit"                       | Benefit-driven, first-person           |

## Service Pages

Each service page hero should answer four questions within the first scroll:

1. **What problem do we solve?** — Lead with the client's pain, not your service name
2. **How is our approach different?** — One specific differentiator, backed by a number
3. **What results can you expect?** — A specific, credible stat
4. **What is the next step?** — One clear CTA with a low-commitment ask

## About Page

**Problem:** Most about pages talk about the agency. Clients don't care — they want to know what the agency does _for them_.

**Recommended structure:**

1. **Hero:** Lead with client outcome ("We help [X type of business] do [specific result]"), not "We were founded in…"
2. **Story:** Keep it short (3–4 sentences). Mention the year (2015), the city (Hurghada), the specific market insight that led to founding.
3. **Team:** Emphasize depth ("22 full-time specialists — not freelancers") and local knowledge
4. **Values:** 3 values max, each backed by a specific behavior ("We show up in person — every client gets a dedicated account manager they can call")

## Blog Post Titles

Avoid: Generic topic labels ("Social Media Tips", "SEO Guide")

Use instead:

- Specific numbers: "7 SEO Mistakes Hurghada Hotels Make (And How to Fix Them)"
- Outcome-led: "How We Increased a Resort's Direct Bookings by 68% in 6 Months"
- Contrarian: "Why Most Red Sea Businesses Are Wasting Their Social Media Budget"
- Local hook: "The MENA Marketer's Guide to Ramadan Campaign Timing"

## Trust Signals Missing Site-Wide

Add these to the homepage and key service pages:

- [ ] Client count with industry breakdown ("Trusted by 50+ brands in tourism, hospitality, retail, and real estate")
- [ ] Founded year prominently ("Since 2015")
- [ ] Specific results, not vague claims ("340% avg organic traffic increase", "3.4× avg ROAS", "68% direct booking increase")
- [ ] Team size and structure ("22 full-time specialists — no outsourcing")
- [ ] Local credibility ("Born in Hurghada, serving the Red Sea region and beyond")
- [ ] Named client logos (with permission) or industries served
