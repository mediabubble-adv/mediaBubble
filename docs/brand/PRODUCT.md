# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative professionals, designers, developer implementers, copywriters, and clients/partners of MediaBubble who need to reference, copy, or download brand assets and guidelines.

## Product Purpose

Provides a single source of truth for MediaBubble's brand identity, visual style (logo, typography, colors), and guidelines. It enables seamless usage of assets and enforces brand alignment across Egypt and UAE web apps and marketing campaigns.

## Brand Personality

Sleek, creative, strategic, and professional. We are strategic allies, not vendors. Our tone is bold, inspiring, and outcomes-driven.

## Anti-references

- SaaS-cream / warm neutral default templates (#paper, #sand, beige palettes).
- Saturated AI-slop elements (such as gradient text, excessive glassmorphism, over-rounded card corners > 16px).
- Vague marketing buzzwords (e.g. "synergy-based full-service marketing capabilities").
- Side-stripe card borders and generic flat icon grids.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, Don't Tell**: Visual specs must be interactive and live-rendered, serving as working code examples themselves.
2. **Commit to Dark Depth**: Embrace the deep, dark creative studio dashboard aesthetic with rich blues, glowing yellows, and crisp borders.
3. **Outcome-Oriented Utility**: Prioritize direct productivity (one-click copy-to-clipboard, clear navigation shortcuts, zero-friction asset downloads).
4. **Typographic Authority**: Make font pairing (Poppins + Inter + Cairo) feel deliberate, with strong scale contrast and balanced text wrap.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Strict contrast checks for text on tinted backgrounds (WCAG AA level, minimum 4.5:1 ratio).
- Reduced motion support (`prefers-reduced-motion`) for all dashboard animations.
- Accessible dark/light mode surface switching without flashing.
