## 🎨 TEAM 1: DESIGN UNION (Creative + Brand + UX/UI + Visual)

### **Team Leadership Structure**

| Role | Agent | Responsibilities | Communication Channel |
|------|-------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Design Director** | Design Department Manager | Oversees all design agents, ensures brand consistency, escalates critical issues | #agents-design, #agents-management |
| **Creative Director** | Style & Vision Lead | Sets creative direction, approves visual concepts, maintains design language | #agents-design |
| **Brand Director** | Brand Compliance Lead | Validates all outputs against brand guidelines, enforces voice/tone | #agents-design, #agents-quality |
| **Art Director** | Visual Standards Lead | Reviews visual assets, ensures quality, coordinates design handoffs | #agents-design |

### **Department Agents (5)**

| Agent | Time Saved | Function | Manager Coordination |
|-------|------------|----------|---------------------|
| Design Brief Generator | 30 min/brief | Creates comprehensive design briefs from requirements | Reports to Design Director daily |
| Asset Variations Generator | 2-4 hrs/batch | Batch-generates platform-specific design variations | Receives creative direction from Creative Director |
| Brand Consistency Checker | 1 hr/design | Flags brand guideline violations with suggested fixes | Reports to Brand Director for policy updates |
| Social Media Batching | 1-2 days | Generates cohesive 20-post sets for social campaigns | Coordinates with Art Director for visual quality |
| Mockup Generator | 1 hr/mockup | Creates 3D mockups from designs instantly | Works with Art Director on presentation quality |

### **Communication Flow**

```
Client Request
    ↓
Design Director (analyzes requirements)
    ↓
├── Creative Director sets vision direction
├── Brand Director validates brand compliance
└── Art Director oversees visual quality
    ↓
Design Agents execute (Brief → Variations → Mockups)
    ↓
Quality Check (Brand + Art Directors review)
    ↓
Slack: #agents-design → "✅ Design Brief Complete for Client X"
    ↓
Dashboard: Visual asset uploaded with metadata
```

## 🔄 Cross-Team Communication Protocols

### **Inter-Team Handoff Rules**

| From Team | To Team | Handoff Trigger | Required Approval |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Design | Marketing | New visual assets complete | Art Director |
| Marketing | Lead Gen | Content published | Content Strategist |
| Lead Gen | Sales | Lead score >80 | Growth Director |
| Sales | Management | Deal closed/won | Operations Director |
| Development | All Teams | API/feature deployed | Technical Lead |
| Finance | All Teams | Budget threshold exceeded | Financial Controller |
| Social | Sales | Engagement spike detected | Social Strategy Lead |
| Media Buying | Lead Gen | Campaign launched | Media Optimization Lead |

### **Communication Channels Matrix**

```
#agents-design      → Design Union internal + Art Director review
#agents-social      → Social Strategy Lead coordination
#agents-leads       → Lead Intelligence Lead + Growth Director
#agents-media-buying → Media Optimization Lead + Budget alerts
#agents-development → Technical Lead + all-department tech updates
#agents-marketing   → Content Strategist review
#agents-sales       → Growth Director oversight
#agents-management  → Operations Director coordination
#agents-finance     → Financial Controller + budget alerts
#agents-notifications → Critical system-wide alerts
```

## 📊 Performance & Monitoring

### **Team-Level Dashboards**

Each team has dedicated dashboard views:
- **Design Union:** `/dashboard/team/design` — Creative output, brand compliance, visual quality scores
- **Operations:** `/dashboard/team/operations` — Internal efficiency, cost savings, code quality
- **Growth Engine:** `/dashboard/team/growth` — Revenue metrics, lead conversion, campaign ROI

### **Manager Escalation Protocol**

| Severity | Action | Notification |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Low | Agent resolves autonomously | Weekly report summary |
| Medium | Manager reviews + approves | Slack alert to #agents-management |
| High | Director intervention required | Immediate Slack + Email to leadership |
| Critical | Emergency human takeover | SMS + All-channel alert |
