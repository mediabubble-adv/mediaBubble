## Executive Overview

MediaBubble will launch a new **AI Services Division** offering three core service lines:

1. **AI Automation Services** — Automate client business processes with custom AI agents
2. **Custom Agent Development** — Build specialized AI agents for client-specific needs

**Revenue Target:** $500K-$1M/month (Year 1)
**Target Clients:** Enterprise, mid-market, and high-growth startups
**Timeline:** Launch in 90 days
