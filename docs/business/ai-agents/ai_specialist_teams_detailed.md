# MediaBubble AI Specialist Teams - Detailed Breakdown

## Overview

Each department is organized into specialized sub-teams, where each specialist handles a specific domain expertise. This ensures agents are optimized for their exact use case, users get role-specific support, and quality is maintained at a high level.

**Structure:** Department → Specialist Sub-teams → Expert Agents → Quality Control
