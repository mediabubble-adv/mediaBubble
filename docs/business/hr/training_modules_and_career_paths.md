## OVERVIEW & FRAMEWORK

### Training Philosophy

MediaBubble uses a **competency-based, role-specific training framework** designed to:
- Develop foundational skills for Level 1 (New) → Level 5 (Expert)
- Create clear career advancement pathways (Individual Contributor vs. Management)
- Build organizational knowledge and institutional memory
- Reduce onboarding time from 6 weeks to 3 weeks
- Increase internal promotion rates from current baseline to 85%+ annually

### Training Investment Model

**Per-Employee Annual Training Budget:**
- Specialist: $2,500–$4,000
- Team Manager: $5,000–$7,500
- Project Manager: $4,000–$6,000
- QC Lead: $4,500–$6,500
- Department Manager: $7,500–$10,000

**Organization-Wide Training Budget (Year 1):**
- Total Specialist Training: $537,500–$860,000 (215 specialists)
- Management Training: $135,000–$202,500 (63 managers + leads)
- **Total: $672,500–$1,062,500 annually**

### Training Modalities

1. **Onboarding Training** (Weeks 1–3): Role-specific foundational skills
2. **Role-Based Modules** (Months 2–4): Department deep-dive
3. **Skill Advancement Modules** (Monthly): Level progression (1→2→3→4→5)
4. **Specialization Tracks** (Ongoing): Domain expertise development
5. **Leadership Development** (For managers/leads): Team management, strategy, hiring
6. **Cross-Functional Knowledge Exchange** (Quarterly): Department collaboration

### MODULE 1.1: ONBOARDING — Design Fundamentals (New Hires)

**TARGET ROLE:** All Specialists (Level 1→2)  
**DURATION:** 3 weeks  
**DELIVERY:** Hybrid (instructor-led + hands-on)

#### Learning Outcomes

- Proficiency with core tools (Figma, Adobe XD, Protopie, design systems documentation)

#### Curriculum

- Day 1–2: Brand guidelines, design philosophy, client types

- Day 5: Design system walkthrough + QC standards (what "complete" means)

- Week 3 evaluation with Team Manager

- **Practical:** 3 design assignments graded against rubric (80%+ required to pass)

#### Success Metrics
- Onboarding completion time: ≤3 weeks
- First project approval rate: ≥95%
- Mentor/Manager satisfaction: ≥4.5/5
- Tool proficiency: Figma Level 2 (independent use, component creation)

#### Prerequisites
- Portfolio review confirming design fundamentals

## 3. CAREER PROGRESSION PATHS

### Dual-Track Career Model

MediaBubble offers **two distinct career paths**, allowing high performers to advance without forcing them into management:

#### **Track A: Individual Contributor (IC) / Specialist Path**

Advancement through deepening expertise, specialization, and impact:

```
Level 1: Junior Specialist
- Foundational skills
- Supervised work
- Learning-focused
- Salary: $2,500–$3,500/month

Level 2: Specialist
- Independent work on projects
- Growing impact and quality
- Some mentorship
- Salary: $3,500–$5,000/month

Level 3: Senior Specialist
- Complex project leadership
- Mentoring 1–2 junior specialists
- Strategic thinking
- Salary: $5,000–$7,000/month

Level 4: Principal/Lead Specialist
- Industry-level expertise (approaching world-class)
- Mentoring multiple specialists
- Strategy & innovation
- Speaking/visibility
- Salary: $7,000–$10,000/month

Level 5: Expert/Distinguished Specialist
- World-class expertise (industry-recognized)
- Thought leadership
- Mentoring managers and specialists
- Strategic innovation
- Salary: $10,000–$15,000/month
```

**IC Path Benefits:**
- Remain hands-on in craft
- Become go-to expert
- Mentoring without full management burden
- Higher earning potential at senior levels
- Flexibility in work style
- Industry recognition and speaking opportunities

---

#### **Track B: Management / Leadership Path**

Advancement through people leadership, team building, and strategic impact:

```
Level 1: Team Manager
- Lead 4–5 specialists
- Performance management
- Project delivery
- Salary: $5,000–$7,500/month

Level 2: Senior Team Manager / Lead Manager
- Lead multiple teams or high-performing team
- Budget responsibility
- Strategic input
- Salary: $7,500–$10,000/month

Level 3: Project Manager / Senior Project Manager
- Program-level responsibility
- Cross-team coordination
- Client/stakeholder management
- Salary: $6,000–$9,000/month

Level 4: QC Lead / Operations Lead
- Quality/performance oversight
- Specialist development
- Process optimization
- Salary: $7,000–$10,000/month

Level 5: Department Manager / Director
- Lead entire department (6 teams, 33+ people)
- P&L responsibility
- Strategic vision
- Executive team participation
- Salary: $10,000–$18,000/month
```

**Management Path Benefits:**
- Build and develop teams
- Strategic impact at organizational level
- P&L responsibility
- Executive visibility
- Broader organizational influence
- Clear advancement to director/VP roles

---

### Career Path Selection Framework

**Timeline:** Year 1, Quarter 2 (6 months into role)

**Process:**
1. **Self-Assessment:** Specialist reflects on preferences (hands-on work vs. people leadership)
2. **Manager Conversation:** Open discussion about strengths, interests, aspirations
3. **Strengths Assessment:** Evaluate suitability for chosen path
4. **Development Plan:** Create 1–2 year plan to advance on chosen track
5. **Milestone Review:** Quarterly check-ins to assess progress and fit

**Signals of IC Path Strength:**
- Deep expertise / mastery drive
- Enjoys problem-solving and craft
- Mentoring approach: coaching around specific skills
- Prefers fewer, deeper relationships
- Energized by technical challenges
- Industry visibility aspirations

**Signals of Management Path Strength:**
- People development drive
- Enjoys organizational problem-solving
- Mentoring approach: career development and growth
- Energized by team success
- Strategic thinking orientation
- Executive visibility aspirations
