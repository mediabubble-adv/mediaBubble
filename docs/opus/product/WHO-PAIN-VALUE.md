# Who OPUS Is For, What Pain It Heals, and Real Value

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Product:** OPUS inside MediaBubble Launcher

---

## Who We Build OPUS For

### Primary: MediaBubble internal teams

OPUS is first built for **our own agency** — the people running campaigns for Hurghada and regional clients every day:

| Persona | Role today | What OPUS gives them |
|---------|------------|----------------------|
| **Account Manager** | Chases status across Slack, sheets, ad platforms | One command center: brief → tasks → performance |
| **Social Media Specialist** | Manually plans 15–25 posts/week | WeeklySocialPlanning trigger: generate → review → schedule |
| **Paid Media Lead** | Checks Meta + Google separately, adjusts bids by hand | Unified performance review + optimization triggers |
| **Creative / Content** | Rewrites briefs into channel-specific copy | Structured brief → AI-assisted variants (via Launcher AI) |
| **Leadership** | Monthly reports assembled manually | Live usage, ROAS, and campaign health without waiting |

### Secondary: MediaBubble clients (Managed OPUS)

Clients who buy **managed marketing** get visibility into campaign performance and faster execution — without hiring their own ops stack.

### Tertiary: Reseller / white-label (future)

Other agencies running 5–50 person teams who face the same pain at scale — OPUS becomes a monetizable platform tier.

## The Pain We Heal

### 1. Campaign setup takes days, not hours

**Today (without OPUS):**

- Brief review: ~2 hours  
- Strategy alignment: ~3 hours  
- Content creation: ~16 hours  
- Approvals: ~4 hours  
- Manual platform uploads: ~6 hours  
- Scheduling: ~4 hours  

**Total: 35+ hours per campaign** — and that is before optimization.

**With OPUS:** Structured brief → linked campaign → automated content tasks → approval queue → scheduled publishing. Target **under 2 hours** for MVP flows.

### 2. Optimization is manual and daily

Teams spend **5+ hours/day per account** checking Meta, Google, analytics, building reports, and tweaking bids — often reactively, after budget is wasted.

**OPUS heals this** with PerformanceMonitoring triggers, campaign metric snapshots, and AI insights (scale winners, pause underperformers).

### 3. No closed loop from ads to revenue

Agencies cannot easily answer: *Which campaign actually closed the deal?*

Data lives in Meta, Google, HubSpot, spreadsheets — disconnected.

**OPUS heals this** by design: brief → campaign → metrics → CRM sync (HubSpot path) → attribution dashboard (Phase 2).

### 4. Tool sprawl and context switching

MediaBubble already has Launcher (tasks, CRM, finance, AI, chat). Campaign work still happens in **external tabs**.

**OPUS heals this** by living **inside Launcher** — same auth, same clients, same team — not another SaaS login.

### 5. Bilingual market complexity

Egypt (Masri) and UAE (Khaliji) need distinct copy and RTL UX. Generic marketing tools treat Arabic as an afterthought.

**OPUS heals this** by inheriting MediaBubble's i18n, Arabic skill pipeline, and brand tokens from day one.

## What OPUS Is NOT

- **Not a replacement for creative judgment** — human approval stays in the loop  
- **Not a standalone app (for now)** — it is a workflow layer inside Launcher  
- **Not magic ROI** — it automates ops; strategy and brand still matter  
- **Not fully autonomous on day one** — Meta/Google live publishing and HubSpot sync are phased
