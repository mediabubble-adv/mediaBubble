## Negative Construction

| Country | Construction | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Moroccan | ما + verb + ش | ما مشيتش (mā mshīt-esh) "I didn't go" |
| Algerian | ما + verb + ش | ما مشيتش (mā meshīt-esh) |
| Tunisian | ما + verb + ش | ما مشيتش (mā mshīt-esh) |
| Libyan | ما + verb + ش | ما مشيتش (mā mshīt-esh) |

Note: Unlike Levantine, Maghrebi Darija does not drop the ش suffix in any form.
