## Easter Date Calculation — Simplified

**Julian Easter** = First Sunday after the first ecclesiastical full moon on or after 21 March (Julian calendar).

**Gregorian Easter** = Same formula, but with Gregorian equinox. The Julian calendar currently lags 13 days behind Gregorian.

| Year | Gregorian Easter | Julian Easter | Difference |

| 2026 | 5 April | 12 April | 7 days |
