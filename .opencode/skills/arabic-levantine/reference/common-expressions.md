# Levantine — Common Expressions & Idioms by Country

## Shared Levantine Idioms

| Arabic | Literal | Meaning | Used In |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| ع الرايق | On the relaxed | Easygoing; casually | All |
| يخزي العين | Shames the eye | Beautiful; "so good it shames critics" | All |
| من الآخر | From the last | Directly; to the point | All |
| كلة ع بعضو | All on each other | Everything together; all at once | All |
