# Prophetic Narratives — Quran vs Bible Comparison Table

## Major Prophets — Shared Narrative Comparison

| Prophet | Quranic Account | Biblical Account | Key Differences |
|---------|----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Adam** | Created from clay; Iblis refuses to prostrate; Garden; descent to Earth | Genesis: Garden of Eden, serpent, fruit; Adam names animals | Quran: Iblis (not serpent) as tempter; no inherited sin; Adam repents, God forgives |
| **Nuh** | Builds ark; flood; son refuses to board ("ليس من أهلك") | Genesis 6–9: Ark with pairs; son not differentiated | Quran: Nuh's son is separate believer who disobeys; duration less specific |
| **Ibrahim** | Debates Nimrod; smashes idols; thrown in fire (it becomes cool); son sacrifice | Genesis 12–22: Ur → Haran → Canaan; Hagar; Ishmael circumcised; Isaac sacrifice | Quran: 7-year old son (unnamed — Muslims read Ismail); fire miracle; no Sarah laughs |
| **Isma'il** | Helps build Kaaba; patient sacrifice candidate | Genesis: expelled with Hagar; wilderness; Ishmaelites | Quran: named in sacrificial context; central to Mecca foundation |
| **Yusuf** | Full surah (12); Zulaykha seduction; prison; Egypt minister; family reunion | Genesis 37–50: Joseph & brothers; Potiphar's wife; jail; minister; forgiveness | Quran: more detail on Zulaykha, Yusuf's rhetorical speeches; ends with Yusuf's prayer |
| **Musa** | Pharaoh; 10 plagues (detailed); parting sea; golden calf; Khidr/Joshua | Exodus: 10 plagues; more legal detail; wilderness wanderings | Quran: Khidr story (18:60–82) is unique; no Book of Job parallel; magical contest with Pharaoh's magicians emphasized |
| **'Isa** | Virgin birth; miracles (bird of clay); disciples; crucifixion denial; ascension | Gospels: Virgin birth; crucifixion; resurrection; atonement theology | Quran: No crucifixion (God raised him); no divinity; no original sin; named "ابن مريم" 23 times |
