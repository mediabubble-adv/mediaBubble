# Humor & Tone — Egyptian Cultural Boundaries

## Egyptian Humor Profile

Egyptian humor is: **sharp, self-deprecating, situational, verbally clever, and often dark.** It's a coping mechanism in a challenging environment. But humor boundaries are NOT universal across regions, generations, or contexts.

---

## Humor Types by Region

### Cairo — Ironic & Fast

| Type | Example | Acceptable |
|------|---------|------------|
| Irony / sarcasm | "أه .. طبعاً .. النظام زي الفل" | ✓ Yes, common |
| Self-deprecation | "احنا المصريين طبعاً مش بنظبط حاجة" | ✓ Yes, but context matters |
| Situational | Traffic, bureaucracy, family situations | ✓ Yes, most common |
| Verbal wordplay | Puns, double meanings (جناين) | ✓ Yes, very Egyptian |
| Dark humor | About daily struggles, prices, infrastructure | ✓ Yes, but not in health context |

### Alexandria — Laid-back & Warm

| Type | Acceptable? |
|------|-------------|
| Gentle teasing | ✓ Yes |
| Sea/nature references | ✓ Yes |
| Sarcasm | ⚠️ Less than Cairo |
| Dark humor | ⚠️ Use sparingly |

### Nile Delta — Practical & Family

| Type | Acceptable? |
|------|-------------|
| Family jokes | ✓ Yes |
| Work situations | ✓ Yes |
| Irony | ⚠️ Limited — may be misunderstood |
| Dark humor | ❌ No |
| Sarcasm about authority | ❌ No |

### Upper Egypt (Sa'eed) — Direct & Honorable

| Type | Acceptable? |
|------|-------------|
| Gentle, respectful humor | ✓ Yes, careful |
| Self-deprecation | ✓ Yes |
| Irony / sarcasm | ❌ No — perceived as dishonesty |
| Dark humor | ❌ No |
| Authority mockery | ❌ No — extremely offensive |
| Wordplay | ⚠️ Only if clearly appropriate |

## What's Funny vs Offensive

### Safe Humor Topics (All Regions)

| Topic | Why It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| Everyday Egyptian bureaucracy | Universal experience, relatable |
| Family dynamics (in-laws, parenting) | Universal, safe |
| Traffic in Cairo | Everyone suffers together |
| Technology struggles (slow internet, WhatsApp problems) | Relatable across generations |
| Food preferences and arguments | Neutral, warm |
| Sleep deprivation (new parents, exam season) | Shared experience |
| Weather complaints (heat, humidity) | Harmless |

### Risky Humor Topics

| Topic | Risk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Government / politics | HIGH | Can trigger serious backlash |
| Religion | VERY HIGH | Never acceptable in marketing |
| Physical appearance | MEDIUM-HIGH | Body shaming is never ok |
| Economic struggles | MEDIUM | Can feel insensitive if brand seems tone-deaf |
| Gender roles | MEDIUM | Rapidly evolving — get it wrong and face backlash |
| Regional stereotypes | MEDIUM | Funny within community, offensive from outsiders |
| Health conditions | HIGH | Never joke about illness, disability, or pain |
| Mental health | HIGH | Stigma still strong in many regions |

## Humor in Healthcare Content

### Acceptable Healthcare Humor Topics
- Pre-procedure nerves (light, relatable)
- Waiting room experiences
- Misunderstanding medical terms
- Family members giving unsolicited medical advice
- Failing at New Year's health resolutions
- Trying to exercise at home

### NEVER Acceptable Healthcare Humor
- Making fun of patients or their conditions
- Joking about symptoms, pain, or suffering
- Mocking medical professionals
- Trivializing serious conditions (cancer, heart disease, mental health)
- Weight or body shaming humor
- Sexual health jokes
- Death or terminal illness references

### Healthcare Humor Guidelines
```
✓ DO: "كلنا بنقرر نبدأ دايت يوم السبت .. وبندي نفسنا يوم حد"
✗ DON'T: "شكلك محتاج رجيم" (body shaming)
✓ DO: "نص ساعة في انتظار الدكتور .. خلاص بقينا أصدقاء"
✗ DON'T: تريقة على مرضى أو حالاتهم
✓ DO: "أهلك نصحوك باكل تاني .. وانت عارف إنهم غلط"
✗ DON'T: أي هزار عن الأمراض الخطيرة
```

## Tone Calibration by Scenario

| Scenario | Tone | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| Health awareness campaign | Serious + hopeful | "مش هتخسر حاجة لما تطمن على صحتك" |
| Service promotion | Confident + warm | "احنا بنقدملك خدمة مش هتلاقيها في أي مكان تاني" |
| Patient testimonial | Genuine + emotional | "تجربتي مع MediaBubble غيرت حياتي .. مش بشكرهم كفاية" |
| FAQ / education | Clear + reassuring | "كتير منكم بيسأل نفس السؤال .. خلونا نوضح" |
| Community engagement | Light + inclusive | "عايزين نعرف رأيكم .. إيه أكتر حاجة بتخوفك في الكشف الدوري؟" |
| Crisis communication | Calm + authoritative | "احنا هنا عشان نطمنك ونساعدك في أي استفسار" |
| Holiday greeting | Warm + celebratory | "كل سنة وانت طيب .. وصحتك دايمة يا رب" |
| Brand introduction | Proud + welcoming | "احنا MediaBubble .. وجايين عشان نغير وش الصحة في مصر" |
