## Emoji Rules Per Platform

| Platform | Max Per Post | Approved | Notes |
|----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Facebook | 2 | 😂 🤝 🔥 💪 👀 | Use for warmth. Avoid 😱😭 in brand content. |
| Instagram | 1–2 | 😂 👀 🔥 🤝 💀 | Instagram audience expects some emoji personality |
| LinkedIn | 0–1 | 🤝 💡 📊 | 1 in post preview only. Zero in articles. |
| TikTok | 1–3 | 😂 💀 🔥 👀 🫡 | TikTok audience expects high-energy emoji use |
| Email | 0–2 in body | ✅ 📅 🎁 | 1 in subject for promo only. Zero in formal outreach. |
| Landing | 0 | — | No emojis on landing pages. Hurts credibility. |
| WhatsApp | 1–2 | 👋 🤝 ✅ | Opener: 👋 is expected. Close: 🤝 for agreement. |

## Best Posting Times (Egypt Time)

| Platform | Best Times | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Facebook** | 8–10 AM, 2–4 PM, 8–11 PM | Sunday–Thursday best. Friday morning low engagement. |
| **Instagram** | 9–11 AM, 7–10 PM | Sunday–Wednesday peak. Thursday evening also good. |
| **LinkedIn** | 7–9 AM, 12–1 PM, 5–6 PM | Sunday–Thursday only. Weekend (Fri–Sat) very low. |
| **TikTok** | 6–9 PM, 11 PM–1 AM | Evenings and late night. Friday post-prayer. |
| **Email** | 8–10 AM, 2–4 PM | Sunday–Thursday. Tuesday–Wednesday highest open rates. |
| **WhatsApp** | 10 AM–12 PM, 4–7 PM | Avoid before 9 AM and after 8 PM for business. |

## Hashtag Strategy

| Platform | Count | Mix | Placement |
|----------|-------|-----|-----------|
| **Facebook** | 3–5 | 2 broad + 1–2 niche + 1 brand | After post body, on separate lines |
| **Instagram** | 5–10 | 2 broad + 3 mid + 2 niche + 1 brand | After caption, on separate lines |
| **LinkedIn** | 3–5 | 2 broad + 1–2 industry + 1 brand | At end of post. No hashtags in article body. |
| **TikTok** | 2–5 | 1 trending Egyptian + 1–3 broad + 1 brand | In caption. Also use in video text overlay. |
| **X / Twitter** | 1–2 | 1 trend + 1 brand | Keep minimal. Character limit is tight. |
| **Email** | 0 | — | No hashtags in email. |
