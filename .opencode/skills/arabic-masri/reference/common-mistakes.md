# Common Masri Errors — Reference Catalog

## Grammar Errors

### Negation Confusion
| Wrong | Right | Rule |
|-------|-------|------|
| ما عاوزش | مش عاوز | Non-verb negation uses مش, not ما...ش |
| مش بعمل كده | ما بعملش كده | Verb negation needs ما...ش circumfix |
| ما مش راح | مش راح | Double negation; single مش is correct |

### Demonstrative Agreement
| Wrong | Right | Rule |
|-------|-------|------|
| دا بنت | دي بنت | دا = masculine, دي = feminine |
| دول كتاب | دول كتب | دول = plural; noun must be plural |

### Gender Inconsistency
| Wrong | Right | Rule |
|-------|-------|------|
| إنت بتقول إيه يابنت | إنتي بتقولي إيه يابنتي | Feminine address requires consistent feminine verb endings |
| هي راح | هي راحت | He = راح, She = راحت |

## Orthography Errors

| Wrong | Right | Explanation |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| هم (pronounced "humm") + verb | همـ + verb | همـ prefix for present plural, not the pronoun هم |
| ـه vs ــہ | Depends on font | Ta' marbuta and haa' look similar; check character encoding carefully |
| ي vs ى | ي for ya, ى for alif maqsura | Common in brand names; verify intent |

## Arabizi Errors

| Wrong | Right | Rule |
|-------|-------|------|
| Mixing 2 and 3 with q/a | Pick one system | Decide numeral-based (2/3/7) or letter-based (q/a/kh) |
| Capitalization randomness | No caps in Arabic | Don't start sentences with uppercase Latin letters in Arabizi |
| CamelCase in Arabizi | Lowercase only | "ana3mel" not "Ana3mel" |
