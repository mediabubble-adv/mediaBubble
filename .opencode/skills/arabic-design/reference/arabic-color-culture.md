# Arabic Design — Color Culture Across Arab Markets

## Color Meanings by Region & Context

### Green (الأخضر)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| Pan-Arab | Islam, paradise, nature | Flags (Saudi, UAE, Jordan, etc.) |
| North Africa | Agricultural fertility | Food packaging, organic |
| Levant | Nature, olives, prosperity | Tourism, food |
| Gulf | Wealth, tradition, palms | Banks, luxury |
| ⚠️ | Not used in product packaging for perishable goods (associated with rot/mold in some contexts) | Avoid for: brown-related food products |

### Red (الأحمر)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| Gulf | Danger, passion, speed | Automotive, sports |
| Levant | Courage, sacrifice (historical) | Heritage/cultural events |
| Egypt | Energy, joy (wedding tradition) | Weddings, celebrations |
| North Africa | Revolutionary | Political (Algeria, Tunisia flags) |
| ⚠️ | Avoid in contexts where fire/harm means danger | Safety warnings only |

### Blue (الأزرق)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| All | Water, sky, protection | Corporate, technology |
| Gulf | Sea, trade heritage | Banking (Kuwait, UAE) |
| Egypt | Nile, calm, trust | Healthcare, government |
| Levant | Mediterranean | Travel, tourism |
| ⚠️ | Overuse reads as cold or corporate | Add warm • orange accents |

### White (الأبيض)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| All | Purity, peace | Minimalist design, healthcare |
| Gulf | National day dress, tradition | Thobe/cultural contexts |
| Egypt | Clean, modern | Healthcare, real estate |
| ⚠️ | Death mourning in some contexts | Not for festive packaging |

### Black (الأسود)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| All | Elegance, power | Luxury, fashion |
| Gulf | Abbasid heritage, tribal prestige | High-end branding |
| Levant | Mourning primarily | Limited cultural branding |
| ⚠️ | Associated with death and loss | Not for celebration branding |

### Gold (الذهبي)

| Region/Context | Meaning | Use For |
|----------------|---------|---------|
| Gulf | Wealth, luxury, prestige | High-end retail, jewelry |
| Egypt | Pharaohic heritage, royalty | Tourism, luxury |
| Levant | Church ornament, nobility | Religious contexts |
| ⚠️ | Overuse looks cheap | Use as accent • vector element |

## Culture-Specific Color Taboos

| Color | Cultural Context | Avoid |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| Yellow | Egypt, Levant | Jealousy, sickness, cowardice |
| Violet/purple | Conservative Gulf, Levant | Mourning (Christian context); ostentatious in some Gulf states |
| Pink | Saudi Arabia | Until 2018: associated with women only; now more accepted |
| Orange | North Africa | Associated with Berber/Amazigh indigenous political movements in Algeria |
