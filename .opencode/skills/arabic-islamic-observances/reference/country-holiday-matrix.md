## Ramadan Content Implications

| Timing | Content Strategy |
|--------|-----------------|
| Pre-Ramadan (Sha'ban) | "Ramadan prep" — recipes, schedules, ibadah goals |
| Week 1 | High spiritual engagement; Quran, prayer content |
| Week 2 | Fatigue starts; motivational, short-form |
| Week 3 | Mid-Ramadan push; community, charity, Laylat al-Qadr anticipation |
| Last 10 days | Intense ibadah; Laylat al-Qadr content; night content |
| Last 3 days | Eid prep, zakah al-fitr reminders |
| Eid day | Celebration; greetings; family content |

## Key Dua & Supplication Dates

| Night/Occasion | Significance |
|----------------|--------------|
| Laylat al-Qadr | Typically 27th or odd last-10; the most important single night |
| Arafah (9 Dhul Hijjah) | Best day of the year for dua |
| Friday after Asr | Hour of acceptance |
| Last third of night (tahajjud) | Guarded and answered |
