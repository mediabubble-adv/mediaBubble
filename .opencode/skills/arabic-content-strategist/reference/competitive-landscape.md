# Competitive Landscape — Egyptian Healthcare Content Market

## Competitor Categories

### Category 1: Healthcare Directories & Booking Platforms

| Competitor | Focus | Content approach | Masri? | Content gap |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|--------|-------------|
| Vezeeta | Clinic booking, doctor reviews | MSA-heavy, professional, AI-assisted | Partial | Low Masri authenticity, generic content |
| El-Doctoor | Doctor directory | Minimal content, functional | Partial | No content strategy, pure directory |
| Sehhaty (Govt) | Government health services | MSA formal, informational | No | No engagement strategy, institutional tone |
| Yadawy | Home healthcare booking | Transactional content | Yes | Limited content depth, no lifestyle angle |
| Teb Can | Doctor directory | Low content output | Partial | Inactive content channels |

**Opportunity:** None of the competitors own a distinct content voice. Vezeeta has the most reach but lacks authentic Masri engagement. MediaBubble can win on Masri-first content with personality.

### Category 2: Healthcare Information & Media

| Competitor | Focus | Content approach | Masri? | Content gap |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|--------|-------------|
| WebTeb | Health info in Arabic | MSA, comprehensive library | No | No Masri, no Egypt-specific focus, no platform personality |
| Altibbi | Arab world health info | MSA, clinical, broad | No | Arabic but pan-Arab, not localised for Egypt |
| Daily Medical | Egyptian health news | MSA, news-style, clinical | Partial | Dry, no brand personality, low shareability |
| Mayo Clinic (Arabic) | Global content translated | MSA translated content | No | Zero local relevance, translated, not Egyptian |

**Opportunity:** No competitor creates health content in Masri dialect. This is a blue ocean. The closest is Daily Medical but their content is news-formatted without brand voice or engagement strategy.

### Category 3: Medical Tourism Platforms

| Competitor | Focus | Content approach | Masri? | Content gap |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|--------|-------------|
| Egypt Med Tour | Medical tourism | English-first, some Arabic | No | Low digital presence, outdated content |
| Treatment Abroad | Global medical tourism | English, generic | No | Not Egypt-specific |
| Medical Egypt | Treatment packages | Outdated website, basic content | Partial | No content strategy, poor UX |

**Opportunity:** Medical tourism content in the Egyptian market is severely underdeveloped. There is room to own this niche by creating high-quality, Masri+bilingual content targeting Gulf and African audiences.

### Category 4: General Health & Lifestyle Brands

| Competitor | Focus | Content approach | Masri? | Content gap |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|--------|-------------|
| نون | Baby & parenting | Egyptian dialect, authentic voice | Yes | Limited to parenting, not general health |
| سوبر ماما | Parenting & wellness | Egyptian dialect, community-driven | Yes | No healthcare services angle |
| Influencer doctors | Personal branding | Varies, some Masri | Partial | Inconsistent, personal brand not scalable |

**Opportunity:** These brands prove that Masri content drives engagement. The gap is a healthcare-specific brand with the same dialect authenticity.

## Monitoring Setup

### Tools
- **Social listening:** Brand24 or Awario (Arabic language support)
- **Competitor tracking:** Monthly manual audit + SEMRush for keyword overlap
- **Share of voice:** Track mentions of MediaBubble vs Vezeeta, El-Doctoor, Daily Medical
- **Keyword monitoring:** Google Search Console for Arabic healthcare queries
