# Campaign Frameworks — Egyptian Healthcare Market

## Campaign Types

### 1. Awareness Campaign — "اكتشف"

**Goal:** Introduce a healthcare topic, service, or provider to a broad audience
**Funnel stage:** Top of funnel
**Duration:** 7-14 days
**Platform mix:** Instagram + TikTok + Facebook
**KPIs:** Reach, impressions, new followers, video views, share rate

| Day | Content | Platform | Format | Register |
|-----|---------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Hook teaser: "معلومة هتغير نظرتك للصحة في مصر" | Instagram | Reel | R2 |
| 3 | Deep dive: topic explainer carousel | Facebook | Carousel | R2 |
| 5 | Short version: 15s key stat | TikTok | Video | R2 |
| 7 | Engagement: "إيه رأيك؟" poll/story | Instagram | Stories | R2 |
| 10 | Expert take: doctor commentary | Instagram | Reel | R1 |
| 14 | Roundup: full guide on website | Website | Article | R1 |

**Masri hook examples:**
- "فيه حاجة في الصحة في مصر ماحدش بيقولك عليها"
- "لو مكنتش تعرف المعلومة دي، يبقى ضيعت كتير"
- "مصر فيها حاجة إسمها .. هتصدمك"

---

### 2. Consideration Campaign — "قارن واختار"

**Goal:** Help prospects evaluate options and choose a provider or service
**Funnel stage:** Middle of funnel
**Duration:** 10-14 days
**Platform mix:** Facebook + LinkedIn + Instagram
**KPIs:** Click-through rate, landing page visits, guide downloads, booking inquiries

| Day | Content | Platform | Format | Register |
|-----|---------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Pain point: "مشكلة كل مريض في مصر" | Facebook | Post | R2 |
| 3 | Solution: comparison guide | Website | Guide | R1 |
| 5 | Social proof: patient testimonial | Instagram | Video | R2 |
| 7 | Data: facts and figures carousel | LinkedIn | Carousel | R1 |
| 10 | Expert Q&A: live session | Facebook | Live | R1 |
| 12 | Special offer or free consultation | Instagram | Post | R2 |
| 14 | Reminder: "لسه متردتش؟" | All | Short video | R2 |

**Masri hook examples:**
- "مش عارف تختار دكتور ولا مستشفى؟ احنا هنرشحلك"
- "قارنا أحسن المستشفيات في مصر عشان تختار الصح"
- "ناس كتير بتقع في الغلط ده .. احنا هنقلك إزاي تختار صح"

---

### 3. Conversion Campaign — "احجز دلوقتي"

**Goal:** Drive bookings, consultations, or sign-ups
**Funnel stage:** Bottom of funnel
**Duration:** 7-10 days
**Platform mix:** Instagram + Google Ads + Facebook
**KPIs:** Conversion rate, cost per acquisition, booking count, revenue

| Day | Content | Platform | Format | Register |
|-----|---------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Urgency: limited-time offer | Instagram | Story | R2 |
| 3 | Trust: doctor/profile spotlight | Instagram | Carousel | R1 |
| 5 | FOMO: "آخر فرصة" | Facebook | Post | R2 |
| 7 | Social proof: results/numbers | Instagram | Reel | R2 |
| 10 | Final push: "العرض بينتهي" | All | Short video | R2 |

**Masri hook examples:**
- "العرض لآخر النهاردة .. احجز استشارتك دلوقتي"
- "أول 50 شخص يحجزوا يحصلوا على خصم"
- "متضيعش الفرصة .. الكشف مجاناً لفترة محدودة"

---

### 4. Retention Campaign — "احنا معاك"

**Goal:** Maintain engagement with existing patients/customers, encourage repeat visits
**Funnel stage:** Post-conversion
**Duration:** Ongoing (monthly touchpoints)
**Platform mix:** Email + WhatsApp + Facebook
**KPIs:** Repeat booking rate, referral rate, NPS, email open rate

| Touchpoint | Content | Channel | Frequency |
|------------|---------|---------|-----------|
| Post-treatment | "شكراً لثقتك .. عامل إيه دلوقتي؟" follow-up | WhatsApp | Day 1, Day 7, Day 30 |
| Monthly newsletter | Health tips, new services, community updates | Email | Monthly |
| Birthday | Special offer + message | WhatsApp | Annual |
| Referral request | "لو عجبك خدمتنا، قول لصاحبك" | WhatsApp | Day 30 |
| Re-engagement | "افتقدناك .. تعالى كده نشوفك" | Facebook | Quarterly |

**Masri hook examples:**
- "إزاي صحتك النهاردة؟ احنا فاكرينك"
- "عاوزين نطمن عليك .. كلمنا لو محتاج حاجة"
- "صاحبك محتاج دكتور ثقة؟ قوله على MediaBubble"

---

### 5. Seasonal Campaign — "موسم"

**Goal:** Capitalize on Egyptian cultural moments and seasons
**Funnel stage:** Awareness + Consideration
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks (pre-season through peak)
**Platform mix:** All platforms
**KPIs:** Reach, engagement, campaign-specific actions

| Season | Campaign Angle | Content Series | Timing |
|--------|---------------|----------------|--------|
| Ramadan | "رمضان صحي" | Fasting tips, family health, spiritual wellness | Pre-Ramadan → Eid |
| Summer | "صيفك صحي" | Medical tourism push, summer health | June → August |
| Back-to-School | "صحة أولادك" | Children's health, school checkups | August → September |
| White Friday | "عروض صحتك" | Discounted health packages, limited offers | November |
| Winter | "شتا دافي صحي" | Winter wellness, immune boost, chronic care | December → January |

**Seasonal Masri hooks:**
- "رمضان فرصة تبدأ حياة صحية جديدة"
- "الصيف ده .. خد إجازة وراجع صحتك"
- "أولادك أغلى حاجة .. اطمن عليهم قبل المدرسة"

## Funnel-Stage Content Matrix

| Funnel Stage | Goal | Content Types | Platforms | Register | KPI |
|-------------|------|--------------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Awareness | Introduce | Reels, short videos, hooks, infographics, polls | Instagram, TikTok, Facebook | R2 | Reach, views, shares |
| Consideration | Educate | Carousels, articles, guides, comparisons, webinars | Facebook, LinkedIn, Website | R1 + R2 | CTR, time on page, downloads |
| Conversion | Act | Landing pages, offers, testimonials, consultations | Instagram, Facebook, Google Ads | R2 | Conversion rate, bookings |
| Retention | Nurture | Newsletters, follow-ups, birthday offers, referrals | Email, WhatsApp | R1 | Repeat bookings, referrals |
| Advocacy | Amplify | UGC, reviews, case studies, community | Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn | R1 + R2 | NPS, shares, reviews |
