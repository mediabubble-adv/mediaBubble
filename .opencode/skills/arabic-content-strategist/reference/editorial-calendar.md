**Duration:** 30 days (March 2025)

| Phase | Week | Theme | Content per week | Platform |
|-------|------|-------|-----------------|----------|
| Pre-Ramadan | Pre-week 1 | Ramadan prep | 3 posts: tips, checklists, recipes | Facebook, Instagram |
| Week 1 | Week 1 | Family health routines | 4 posts + 2 Reels | Facebook (prime), Instagram |
| Week 2 | Week 2 | Chronic care during fasting | 3 posts + 1 article + 1 Reel | Facebook, LinkedIn |
| Week 3 | Week 3 | Medical tourism: Umrah + treatment | 3 posts + 2 Reels | Instagram, TikTok |
| Week 4 | Week 4 | Eid preparation | 3 posts + 1 article | Facebook, Instagram |
| Post-Ramadan | Post-week 1 | Eid greetings, post-fast health | 3 posts + 2 Reels | Instagram, Facebook |

**Ramadan posting schedule:** 9 PM – 12 AM (iftar to sohour window), peak engagement 10:30 PM

## Mini-Campaign Structure

Each mini-campaign: 4-6 pieces over 7-10 days

```
Day 1 - Hook: Teaser Reel (Instagram)
Day 2 - Deep dive: Carousel or Article (Facebook / LinkedIn)
Day 3 - Social proof: Testimonial / Case study (Instagram)
Day 4 - Engagement: Poll / Quiz / Q&A (Instagram Stories)
Day 5 - Reminder: Short video with CTA (TikTok)
Day 6 - Close: Wrap-up post + results (Facebook / LinkedIn)
```
