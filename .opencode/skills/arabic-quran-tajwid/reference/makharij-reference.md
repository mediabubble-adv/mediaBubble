# Tajwid — Complete Makharij Reference (Articulation Points)

## The 5 Major Articulation Areas

### 1. Al-Jawf (Oral Cavity) — الجوف

| Makhraj | Letters | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| From the oral cavity | أ, و, ي (مَــدّ) | Three letters of madd — prolonged from the chest cavity |

### 2. Al-Halq (Throat) — الحلق

| Depth | Makhraj | Letters | Special Notes |
|-------|---------|---------|--------------|
| Deepest (Aqsa al-Halq) | اقصى الحلق | ء (hamza), هـ (hā') | Hamza is the furthest throat letter |
| Middle (Wasṭ al-Halq) | وسط الحلق | ع ('ayn), ح (ḥā') | ع has no equivalent in English |
| Lowest (Adna al-Halq) | أدنى الحلق | غ (ghayn), خ (khā') | Both heavy; use back tongue mouth |

### 3. Al-Lisān (Tongue) — اللسان (10 points, 18 letters)

| Point | Tongue Part | Letters | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|---------|
| Back (deep) | Deepest tongue vs. soft palate | ق (qāf) | "قل هو الله أحد" |
| Back (front) | Front of deep tongue vs. hard palate | ك (kāf) | "الله أكبر" |
| Mid | Middle tongue vs. hard palate | ش (shīn), ج (jīm), ي (yā' non-madd) | "شجرة" |
| Side | Side of tongue vs. molars | ض (ḍād) | "الضالين" |
| Tip + near | Tip near front vs. upper gum | ل (lām) | "الله" |
| Tip + front | Tip vs. front upper gum (molars) | ن (nūn) | "نور" |
| Tip | Tip of tongue vs. upper front gums | ر (rā') | "رب" |
| Tip + middle | Tip vs. upper front teeth | ط (ṭā'), د (dāl), ت (tā') | "طالب" |
| Tip + upper | Tip vs. upper teeth edges | ص (ṣād), س (sīn), ز (zāy) | "صبر" |
| Tip + lower | Tip vs. lower front teeth | ظ (ẓā'), ذ (dhāl), ث (thā') | "ظلم" |

### 4. Ash-Shafatān (Lips) — الشفتان

| Makhraj | Letters | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Inside lower lip vs. upper incisors | ف (fā') | Drawn in |
| Between both lips | ب (bā'), م (mīm), و (wāw) | Full closure for ب and م |

### 5. Al-Khayshūm (Nasal Cavity) — الخيشوم

| Letter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Ghunnah (الغنة) | Sound from nasal passage; applies to م and ن when idghām, ikhfā, or iqlāb |
