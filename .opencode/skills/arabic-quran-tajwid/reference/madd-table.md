# Tajwid — All 12 Types of Madd with Examples

## Original (Natural) Madd — المد الأصلي (الطبيعي)

| Type | Length | Condition | Example |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Madd Aṣlī (Tabīʿī) | 2 counts | No hamza or sukūn | "قال" (qāla), "يقول" (yaqūlu), "رميم" (ramīm) |
| Madd Tabīʿī (Substitute) | 2 counts | Waqf on tanwīn | "عليماً" → "عليمَا" |
| Madd Tabīʿī (Silent) | 2 counts | Letter of madd followed by original | "الهاكم" |

## Madd Due to Hamza — المد بسبب الهمزة

| Type | Length | Condition | Example |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| 1. Muttasil | 4–5 counts | Hamza after madd in same word | "السماء" (al-samā'u) |
| 2. Munfaṣil | 4–5 counts | Hamza after madd in next word | "يا أيها" (yā ayyuhā) |
| 3. Badal | 2 counts | Hamza before madd letter | "آدم" → originally "أأدم" |

## Madd Due to Sukūn — المد بسبب السكون

| Type | Length | Condition | Example |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| 4. 'Āriḍ li-s-Sukūn | 2/4/6 counts | Madd + letter, stop at word end | "العالمين" (stopping) |
| 5. Lāzim (Kalimī Thaqīl) | 6 counts | Heavy — shadda + madd in word | "الضالين" |
| 6. Lāzim (Kalimī Mukhaffaf) | 6 counts | Light — sukoon (no shadda) + madd | "آلآن" |
| 7. Lāzim (Ḥarfī) | 6 letters per | Starts surah letter groups | "الم" → ا ل م |
| 8. Lāzim (Ḥarfī Mukhaffaf) | 2 or 6 | Letters with madd, no shadda in letter | "طسم" → ط س م |
| 9. Lāzim (Ḥarfī Thaqīl) | 6 | Letters with shadda | "المص" → ص |
| 10. Li-l-Līn | 2/4/6 | Wāw or yā sākina followed by stop | "البيت" → "البَيْتْ" (stopping) |

## Warning: Common Madd Errors

| Error | Correct | Rule |
|-------|---------|------|
| Over-prolonging Tabīʿī (3+ counts) | 2 counts only | Tabīʿī is always 2 — never more |
| Under-prolonging Muttasil (2–3 counts) | 4–5 counts | Must be longer than Tabīʿī |
| Forgetting 'Āriḍ allows 2 counts | Optional 2/4/6 | Most reciters use 4; all three are valid |
| Mixing 4 and 6 counts mid-verse | Pick one style (Ḥafṣ/Shu'bah etc.) | Stay consistent within one recitation |
