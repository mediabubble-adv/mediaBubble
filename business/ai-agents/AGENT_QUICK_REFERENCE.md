## RED FLAGS TO ADDRESS

### **"Isn't this just a chatbot?"**
> "No. A chatbot answers pre-written questions. Our agent understands context, makes decisions, integrates with your systems (POS, EHR, CRM), and learns from interactions. It's autonomous."

### **"Won't customers want to talk to a human?"**
> "Our agent handles 80% of requests perfectly. The remaining 20% get escalated to a human instantly. You're not replacing humans. You're freeing them from repetitive work so they can do higher-value stuff."

### **"What if the agent makes a mistake?"**
> "All agent responses are logged and reviewed. We monitor for errors and refine responses. Plus, critical decisions (like medical advice) always escalate to a human."

### **"How long to set up?"**
> "2-4 weeks depending on complexity. We integrate with your systems, train the agent on your business, then deploy and monitor for month 1."
