# MediaBubble Complete Organizational Structure

## Executive Overview

**Organization Structure:** Executive Level → Department Managers → Project Managers → Teams (with Team Managers) → Specialists → Quality Control

**Classification:** Departments are categorized by primary stakeholder:
- **Client-Facing Departments** — Directly generate client value
- **Internal Operations Departments** — Support MediaBubble operations
- **Hybrid Departments** — Serve both clients and MediaBubble

---

## ORGANIZATIONAL HIERARCHY

```
CEO/Founder (Dorgham)
    ↓
Chief AI Operations Officer (C-AI)
    ├─ CLIENT-FACING DEPARTMENTS (4)
    │   ├─ Design Department
    │   ├─ Social Media Marketing Department
    │   ├─ Lead Generation Department
    │   └─ Media Buying Department
    │
    ├─ INTERNAL OPERATIONS DEPARTMENTS (3)
    │   ├─ Development Department
    │   ├─ Operations/Management Department
    │   └─ Finance Department
    │
    └─ HYBRID DEPARTMENTS (2)
        ├─ Marketing Department (Brand + Client Content)
        └─ Sales Department (Internal + Client Deals)
```
